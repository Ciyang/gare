Instructions

Each time you will see two images. One of them is in a solid red box and the other is in a dotted black box. Now imagine that your friend sees the same two images, but both of them are in dotted black boxes. Your task is to complete a sentence requesting your friend to click on the image in the solid red box, by choosing one of the options provided to you.

Depending on your Internet connection, some images might take a little longer to load. So if you see the loading icon as shown below, please be patient and wait for the images to load. Thank you!

When you are ready, please click on "Continue" to see an example and get familiar with this task.
