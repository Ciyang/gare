Instructions

Imagine that you and your friend both see two objects. Your friend says a sentence asking you to click on one of the objects and your task is to click accordingly.

However, sometimes you might find the request problematic. That is, it is possible that clicking on neither object seems appropriate to you. If so, please choose an alternative response indicating why you think the request is problematic.
